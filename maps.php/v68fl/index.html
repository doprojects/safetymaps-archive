Safety Map by Dinah

In case of an earthquake, let’s meet at Patricia's Green. I’ve marked the spot on this map:

This Safety Map was made on 4 Feb 2012.

Dinah's plan: If I'm able, will respond as a NERT. Find me or message from me at staging area on the Green. If unable/resting & home is safe, will be there. If home & the Green unsafe, will go to Market & Dolores. Joe's plan: If in FiDi, will shelter at DT if I can, even overnight if streets are dangerous. Otherwise will shelter in place until safe to come to Hayes Valley. Then, as above. If out of SF, will stay safe where I am & await contact. Both will check in with parents if possible.
