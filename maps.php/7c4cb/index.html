Safety Map by Hayes Valley NERTs

In case of catastrophic incident (earthquake etc.), let’s meet at Patricia's Green, Hayes Valley. I’ve marked the spot on this map:

This Safety Map was made on 24 Mar 2011.

Be safe. Don't become a victim. Put on safety gear (mask, gloves, long pants, comfortable sturdy shoes, helmet). Assess & treat self & household. Check on near neighbors. Grab NERT go kit including NERT ID & come to the staging area assembly point. Note neighborhood conditions on the way. If you are first to arrive, establish incident command & assess condition of immediate area. Is this safe for staging area? No hurt NERTs!

PDF downloads are emailed to the sender and recipients of each Safety Map. Make your own!
